Our Story

Rooted in Excellence,
Growing Toward the Future

Founded in 1985 by visionary educator Dr. Arjun Sharma, Northgate Academy began as a small school with 80 students and a singular mission: provide world-class education to every child, regardless of background.

Today, we are one of Nepal's most respected educational institutions — home to over 1,800 students, 120 expert faculty members, and a legacy of graduates who lead in medicine, law, technology, business, and public service across the globe.

Our CBSE and Cambridge curricula are delivered with innovation and care, preparing students not just for exams, but for life's greatest challenges and opportunities.
